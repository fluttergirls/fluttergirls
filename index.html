Welcome to Flutter Girls

Free programming workshop for women

What is Flutter Girls?

Flutter Girls is a community that trains women to become Flutter developers by organizing free programming trainings, providing resoruces and support. Our members are volunteers from all over the world and we are all contributing our quota to have more women in technology. Our learning resources are easy to understand by total programming beginners.

Motivation

Flutter Girls aims to introduce women to the world of technology and increase the diversity. Flutter is a UI toolkit for building natively compiled applications for mobile, web, and desktop from a single codebase. This is a great technology to start with. We believe this is a win-win situation!

Operation

We plan various events and activities in which the participants build a mobile application with Flutter. We offer guidance to getting your first job as a software developer.

Participation

If you're a committed, motivated woman who wants to step into the Software Development world - your place is with us! Want to help? Please get in touch!
